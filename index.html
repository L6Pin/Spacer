RULES

- This is a turn based game where the player and the enemy take turns to fire at each other.

- The player can only shoot the laser and the rocket once per turn. If the player fired a rocket, untill the end of the game he will have no more rockets left.

- After the player has ended their turn, the enemy AI will fire back at you with it's own weapons.

- If you or the enemy get hit by a laser, there is chance that the engines will be disabled, meaning that who ever has their engines destroyed will be 20% more likely to be hit.
